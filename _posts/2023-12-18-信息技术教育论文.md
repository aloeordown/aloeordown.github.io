**Paper：**

1. [Using a teacher scheme for educational dialogue analysis to investigate student–student interaction patterns for optimal group activities in an artificial intelligence course](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10639-022-11556-w#Sec27) 研究探索了小组学习方式开展人工智能课的最佳互动模式。

2. [Teaching artificial intelligence in K–12 classrooms: a scoping review](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/10494820.2023.2212706)

3. A large-scale study based on topic modeling to determine the research interests and trends on computational thinking：在计算思维领域的热点研究：“基于游戏的学习”、“编程技能”和“幼儿编码”，近年来，STEM 领域将计算（即 CT）融入学科教育的趋势日益明显，事实上，由于机器人活动对综合 STEM 教育的贡献，学生可以通过相关 STEM 主题的概念和实践找到现实生活问题的解决方案。美国、中国、西班牙研究居多。

4. Erasmus S, van Eeden R, Ferns I. Classroom factors that contribute to emotional intelligence in the case of primary school learners[J]. South African Journal of Childhood Education, 2022, 12(1): 1072.

   调查课堂气氛对南非小学生情商（EI）水平的影响。满意度和凝聚力与情商EI水平呈正相关，而摩擦力、竞争力和难度与 EI 水平呈负相关。

5. [Experiential learning in entrepreneurship education: A systematic literature review](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0742051X22002943) 体验式学习在创业教学中占据重要地位，被认为是学生边做边学的教学方法。体验式学习的概念以及对于创客教育的好处。

6. [Student motivation and engagement in maker activities under the lens of the Activity Theory: a case study in a primary school](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s40692-023-00258-y)。证明了创客教育的一系列好处：学生认为创客活动易于使用、有用、令人满意、令人愉快，因此具有激励性。创客活动在行为、情感认知和社交参与方面具有吸引力。当创客空间的组成部分被认为是简单、有用、令人满意和愉快时，学生会更有动力使用它。

7. [Learning to innovate: Students and teachers constructing collective innovation practices in a primary school’s makerspace](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/feduc.2022.936724/full) 师生集体实践促进创客创新。

8. [Challenges and Opportunities to Teaching Inquiry Approaches by STE(A)M Projects in the Primary Education Classroom](https://eric.ed.gov/?id=EJ1382839) STEM的好处和难点。
